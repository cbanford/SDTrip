Notable Notes to Note
Tips from the front desk + things to know
🔥
18 public beach bonfire pits — first come first serve along the beach. Bring your own wood, lighter, and supplies. No need to book these ones.
🚲
Front desk recommends the 4–6 seater bicycles — first come first serve. Great for the whole group with the little ones. Head to the marina rental desk early to grab them.
🛝
Ski Beach Park is right across the street — jungle gym playground, easy walk from the resort. Great toddler energy-burner when you don't want to go far.
🏊
Best pools for kids: Meadow Pool or Main Pool — front desk rec. Bring your own pool toys, the resort doesn't provide them.

💡 Key Reminders

🐼 Zoo: PandasDownload the SD Zoo app before you go. Reserve panda timed entry the moment you walk through the gate — they sell out fast.
🎟️ Zoo: TicketsBuy online in advance. Costco members get a discount. Stroller rentals at Front Street Rentals near entrance.
💤 Zoo: Nap HackLoad toddlers into strollers + hop the Kangaroo Bus around noon. Kids doze while you stay in the zoo.
🔥 BonfireBook with the Paradise Point concierge before arrival. Plan for Tuesday evening at dusk. Call (858) 274-4630.
🎡 Balboa Park HoursMiniature Railroad is typically Sat–Sun only. Tuesday March 31 is a weekday — call ahead to confirm or plan an alternate activity.
🏝️ Check-In StrategyOfficial check-in is 4pm. Request early check-in in advance. Bags can be stored anytime — plan to hit the beach immediately on arrival.
🚤 Marina BookingSpring break = peak season. Book watersports as soon as you arrive or call (858) 581-5939 ahead of time.
